# Facility Layout Standards & Requirements

Research-based dimensions, safety zones, and regulatory requirements for specialty recreation facilities.

## Paintball Fields

### Field Dimensions
- **Tournament (5-man)**: 150' x 75' minimum
- **NXL regulation**: 150' x 120'
- **Recreational minimum**: 40' x 40'
- **Woodsball**: 1-5 acres

### Safety Zones
- **Buffer zone**: 375 feet minimum from field perimeter
- **Netting height**: 15 feet minimum
- **Safe zone (staging)**: 50' x 50'

### Supporting Facilities
- Registration/Pro Shop: 400-800 sq ft
- Air fill station: 100 sq ft
- Canteen: 300-600 sq ft
- Bathrooms: 2 stalls minimum
- Parking: 1 space per 4 players

## Airsoft Fields

### Field Dimensions
- **CQB**: 100' x 150'
- **Outdoor tactical**: 200' x 300' to 500' x 500'
- **Indoor warehouse**: Minimum 10,000 sq ft

### Safety Zones
- **Buffer zone**: 300 feet minimum
- **Safe zone**: 30' x 30'

### Supporting Facilities
- Registration/Shop: 500-1000 sq ft
- Parking: 1 space per 3 players

## Trampoline Parks

### Court Dimensions
- **Main court**: 8,000-15,000 sq ft minimum
- **Dodgeball court**: 40' x 70'
- **Foam pit**: 20' x 30'
- **Ceiling height**: 22-24 feet ideal

### Safety Requirements
- **Indoor only** (weather protection)
- **Wall padding**: 6-8 feet up
- **Safety perimeter**: 5 feet around attractions

### Supporting Facilities
- Registration/retail: 600-1000 sq ft
- Party rooms: 200-300 sq ft each (2-4 rooms)
- Canteen: 400-800 sq ft
- Bathrooms: 4 stalls minimum
- Parking: 1 space per 3 visitors

## Gellyball Fields

### Field Dimensions
- **Standard field**: 60' x 80'
- **Kids field**: 40' x 60'

### Safety Zones
- **Buffer zone**: 200 feet (lower velocity)
- **Netting height**: 12 feet
- **Safe zone**: 30' x 30'

### Supporting Facilities
- Registration: 300-500 sq ft
- Bathrooms: 2 stalls
- Parking: 1 space per 5 players

## Family Entertainment Center (FEC)

### Attraction Mix (20,000-40,000 sq ft typical)
- **Arcade**: 3,000-6,000 sq ft
- **Laser tag**: 3,000-5,000 sq ft
- **Go-karts**: 15,000-25,000 sq ft
- **Mini golf**: 5,000-8,000 sq ft

### Supporting Facilities
- Registration/POS: 800-1,200 sq ft
- Party rooms: 300 sq ft each (4-8 rooms)
- Café: 1,000-2,000 sq ft
- Bathrooms: 6-10 stalls
- Parking: 1 space per 2 visitors

## Budget Breakpoints

### Low Budget (<$100k)
- Porta-potties ($150-300/month)
- Connex containers for shop ($3-5k)
- Canopy tents ($500-2k)
- Gravel parking ($2-5/sq ft)

### Medium Budget ($100k-$300k)
- Modular buildings ($15-30k)
- Permanent bathrooms ($40-60k)
- Partial paved parking ($3-8/sq ft)

### High Budget ($300k+)
- Custom buildings ($100-200/sq ft)
- Full amenities (HVAC, plumbing)
- Complete paving and landscaping
